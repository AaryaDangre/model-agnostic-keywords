## 1. Model-agnostic prompting keywords (work across most AI models)

**Clarity and Specificity**
- Be clear and specific
- Provide context
- Define terms
- Specify constraints
- State desired format

**Examples**
- Provide examples
- Show desired output
- Include edge cases
- Demonstrate format

**Step-by-Step Reasoning**
- Think step by step
- Show your work
- Explain reasoning
- Break down complex tasks

**Role Assignment**
- Act as [role]
- You are [expertise]
- Assume the perspective of

**Output Format**
- Format as [type]
- Structure response as
- Output in [format]
- Length: [specification]

**Chain of Thought**
- Explain your reasoning
- Work through this systematically
- Consider alternatives

**Few-Shot Learning**
- Here are examples
- Follow this pattern
- Similar to these cases

**Constraints**
- Do not include
- Avoid [specific content]
- Must include
- Limit to [parameter]

**Persona/Style**
- Tone: [formal/casual/technical]
- Style: [concise/detailed/creative]
- Audience: [beginner/expert/general]

**Iterative Refinement**
- First draft then improve
- Review and revise
- Check for [criteria]

**Decomposition**
- Break this into steps
- Identify sub-tasks
- Address each component

**Verification**
- Double-check your answer
- Verify against [criteria]
- Ensure accuracy
